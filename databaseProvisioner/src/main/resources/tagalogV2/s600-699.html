Bitiwan ritwal, maging doktrina,
Di sa gawi at titik kumapit,
Sa ilog ng buhay manatili.
Di na sa relihiyon, habulin ang tunay,
O, Aleluya! Magsalamuha!

3. Sa ekklesia tayo'y naglilingkuran,
Kristo kainin, lason iwasan;
Sa harapan Niya, buháy ngang batô,
Ang saserdote, tinayong templo.
Hain lugod ng Diyos, sa loob ni Kristo,
O, Aleluya! Naglilingkuran!

4. Sa Kanyang pag-ibig, nagkakaisa,
Mag-unawaan, bigkis payapa.
Isa'y may galak, lahat natuwa;
Isa'y may lungkot, lahat luluha.
Mangagtulungan, isang puso't isipan,
O, Aleluya! Nagkakaisa!

5. Sa lo'b ng kampo Niya, makipagbaka,
Pinanindigan, batayang lupa,
Ang kutamaya, ibihis buo,
Sa ngalang Hesus, daigin diyablo.
Lahat ng panahon, dasal walang hinto,
O, Aleluya! Makipagbaka!

6. Sa kal'walhatian, Kristo'y kasama,
Sumpa'y lumipas, pawang payapa.
Tubig at bunga sa kabuhayan;
Nobyo at Nobya nagmamahalan.
Diyos, tao, nagkai-sa, galak walang hanggan,
O, Aleluya! Kristo'y kasama!

BUILDING AND ONENESS
SEL. 619

1. Lord, burn in us as a consuming fire,
You're burning in our spirit for Your plan;
Lord, now our spirit's filled with your fire,
And this fire must not be limited by man.

2. Lord, we are clear: to us the earth You've given,
And forth we come to take it for Your plan;
Lord, we are bold, without hesitation,
Caleb's extraordinary spirit's ours.

3. From wandering the wilderness we're tired;
We refuse to die like our fathers there.
We're not afraid of giants the land's acquired;
They'll become the bread to fill our inner man!

4. We praise You, Lord, we are the generation
To fulfill Your plan and bring You back again!
We are so strong — we're the Word's habitation
Overcoming the dragon all the time.

5. We give ourselves e'er to exercise our spirit;
Our spirits's made us crazy and on fire.
We're absolute for Your eternal habitation
So we give ourselves completely unto You.

6. We've heard Your call: "Oh who for us will go?
Who'll be sent to fulfill what I said?"
"I'll build my church prevailing o'er the foe
A living church that's not religious, dead!"

7. Lord, use us now according to Your plan;
Lord, we're here to be sent by You today.
Lord, we are those; we've taken your stand;
We are just for You — Oh, send us, Lord, we pray!

BUILDING AND ONENESS
SEL. 625

1. Far from world and all its charms;
Free from sins and all alarms.
Fill me, Lord, my every part;
Change to Thine image, as Thou art.

2. Freed from self, peculiar traits,
And all independent ways,
Lord, I would by Thee be built;
With all saints together built.

3. Built up in love together,
Each one his function render;
Thus the body-life expressed,
And all His glory manifest.

4. By Thy life, we'll be transformed,
Unto precious stones conformed,
Not just individual stones,
But together be built a home.

5. Take my life moments and days,
Let them be used for Thy ways;
For Thy building, I give all,
Let them be under Thy control.
