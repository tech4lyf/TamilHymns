Come Thou and rid my wings.

Thou hast had the "quick" and "slow",
All directions Thou lay hold;
And submitting to Thy way,
Humbly walking in Thy will.

6. Give me boldness to face,
The way Thou hast for me;
Bring all my loves to naught,
And bless with things I hate.

Thou hast had the "quick" and "slow",
All directions Thou lay hold;
And submitting to Thy way,
Humbly walking in Thy will.

7. Oh, spare me not, my Lord,
Nor stand Thou calmly still;
All self-made choices void,
Only Thy will to take.

Thou hast had the "quick" and "slow",
All directions Thou lay hold;
And submitting to Thy way,
Humbly walking in Thy will.
